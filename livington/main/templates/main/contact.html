Frequently Asked Questions About Contacting Us

How quickly will I receive a response to my inquiry?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, contacting us via WhatsApp often provides the fastest response, as our team monitors messages throughout the day. Email inquiries receive detailed responses with relevant property information and documentation. We prioritize timely communication to ensure you never miss out on limited-time opportunities.

Do I need to visit Dubai in person to purchase a property?

While we strongly recommend visiting Dubai to view properties and meet developers in person, it's not absolutely necessary. We can facilitate remote purchases through virtual tours, video calls, and electronic documentation. Our team provides comprehensive visual materials, detailed property information, and can represent you during the purchase process if you cannot travel to Dubai.

Are your services free for buyers?

Yes, our buyer consultation and property search services are completely free. We receive commission directly from developers, so there are no fees or charges for buyers using our services. This allows us to provide unbiased advice and comprehensive support without any financial burden on you. Our goal is to make property investment accessible and straightforward for everyone.

What information should I prepare before contacting you?

To help us provide the most relevant recommendations, consider preparing information about your budget range, preferred locations in Dubai, property type preferences (apartment, villa, townhouse), number of bedrooms needed, and your investment timeline. If you're an investor, knowing your expected ROI goals and whether you plan to rent or resell helps us tailor our suggestions effectively.

Can you help international buyers with visa and residency requirements?

Absolutely. We provide guidance on Dubai's property-linked residency programs, including the requirements for obtaining investor visas and golden visas through property ownership. While we're not immigration lawyers, we work with trusted legal partners who can assist with visa applications, residency permits, and related documentation. We'll connect you with the right professionals based on your specific situation.
